Instructions

1. Add New Artwork

Fill out the form above with your artwork details. The image URL should point to a high-resolution image of your artwork.

2. Preview Your Artwork

Click "Preview" to see how your artwork will look in the gallery before adding it to your site.

3. Copy the HTML

After creating your artwork entry, copy the generated HTML code and paste it into your index.html file within the gallery section.

4. Upload Images

For the best quality, upload your images to:

  • Unsplash - Free high-quality images
  • Your own hosting - Upload to your server or cloud storage
  • Image hosting services - Imgur, Cloudinary, etc.

5. Organizing Your Gallery

Newest artwork should be added at the top of the gallery section in index.html. This ensures it appears first when visitors load your site.
