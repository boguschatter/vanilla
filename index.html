Untitled Composition

A bold exploration of form and color using mixed media on canvas. This piece represents the intersection of chaos and order, created with acrylic paints, charcoal, and recycled paper fragments.

Urban Decay Series #1

Created from found materials and street debris, this piece captures the beauty in urban deterioration. Materials include rusted metal fragments, torn posters, and weathered concrete textures.

Nature's Reclamation

An exploration of how nature reclaims man-made spaces. This sculptural piece incorporates living moss, reclaimed wood, and biodegradable plastics to create a living artwork that evolves over time.

Digital Waste

A commentary on our digital age created entirely from electronic waste. Circuit boards, old smartphones, and cable fragments are woven together to create patterns that mirror neural networks.

Textile Memory

Vintage clothing and fabric scraps tell stories of past lives. Each thread holds memory, woven together with contemporary techniques to bridge generations of textile art and fashion.

Ocean Plastic Dreams

Marine debris transformed into ethereal sculptures. Each piece represents the duality of beauty and destruction in our oceans, created from plastic bottles, fishing nets, and sea glass.
